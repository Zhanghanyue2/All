Tour Highlights

Professional English-speaking guide throughout
Selected 4-star hotel accommodation
Includes main attraction tickets
Daily specialty dining experiences
24 hours emergency support service
1

Auckland - City of Sails

Arrive in Auckland, visit Sky Tower and Harbour Bridge, experience the unique charm of the "City of Sails". Check into downtown hotel to adjust to time difference.

2

Rotorua - Geothermal Wonders

Travel to Rotorua, visit geothermal parks, admire geysers and colorful thermal pools. Experience Maori cultural performances and traditional Hangi cuisine.

3

Wellington - Arts Capital
